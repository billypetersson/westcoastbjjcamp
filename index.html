Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers!

Do I need to bring a gi?

Yes, please bring at least 2 gis as we'll have multiple training sessions per day. We'll also have no-gi sessions, so bring rashguards and shorts/spats. There's laundry service available at the accommodation.

Is the camp beginner friendly?

Absolutely! We welcome practitioners of all levels. Classes are structured to accommodate everyone, with techniques broken down step-by-step. Advanced students will get additional details and variations.

Where do we sleep?

Accommodation is in shared cabins (4-6 people per cabin) at a beautiful facility just 5 minutes from the training venue. Single rooms are available for an additional fee. All cabins have private bathrooms and are very comfortable.

What about meals?

All meals are included and prepared by professional chefs. We offer a variety of healthy options including vegetarian and vegan choices. Please indicate any dietary restrictions when registering.

How do I get to Str√∂mstad?

Str√∂mstad is easily accessible by train from Gothenburg (2 hours) or Oslo (2.5 hours). We can arrange group transfers from Gothenburg airport for those arriving on the same day. Detailed travel information will be sent upon registration.

What's the cancellation policy?

Full refund if cancelled 60+ days before camp. 50% refund if cancelled 30-59 days before. No refund within 30 days of camp start, but you can transfer your spot to another person.
